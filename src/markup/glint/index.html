ABOUT

Post-Internet Era, a long-form documentary narrative, that shows a wide range of stories related to innovative technologies seen as the key point for a new global cycle. Big data, artificial intelligence, cybernetics and behavioral economics are shaping our society—for better or worse. If such widespread technologies are not compatible with our society's core values, sooner or later they will cause extensive damage to the democratic process, worldwide. We are a group of reporters, researchers, data analysts, visual artists and writers, committed to show how fundamental new technologies inherently are for any social, economical and environmental change.

MAIN TOPICS

  • the rising of new e-models for e-governance
  • democratic systems instability and how Internet activism is transforming street politics
  • social effects of communicative fragmentation, the progressive polarization and simplification of general thought
  • new technologies as an hope for the future of environment
  • internet-based business models and their effects on local economies
  • internet of things and big data as the beginning of a synthetic humanity, not only biological anymore
  • accelerationist philosophy in music and art
  • finance speculation in to crypto-currencies and new economic bubbles
  • extreme consequences of climate changes and human impact on the planet
  • the progressive automatization of the workforce
  • the political and economic evolution of high tech oligopolistic companies
  • surveillance and governance with big datas
  • technological evolution of fields like robotics, biotechnologies and biological implementation of synthetic technologies

CODE

Post-Internet Era works with an instant Augmented Reality app, its code is open source so everyone can use and improve it.

The project is a first experiment of a cross-media reportage narrative based over AR. The involved technology is based on open-source projects mainly on AR.js, Three.js and A-Frame.

The technology is all web based that means that is available on all the devices that can access the internet without install an additional app, hence a so called instant app, working absolutely also on mobile devices. The code itself is hosted on Github and is available to be browsed and forked https://github.com/puria/pie

Contact Info

We at Post-Internet Era have decided to open a Submissions section. This gives all of you the possibility to have your work published on post-internet-era.com. Your work may fall into issues related to technology and its impact over human life and environment. We will publish the best of them.

Please, photos should be put in a pdf. Doc and rft are acceptable formats. You can use online hosting spaces like Google Drive, Wetransfer, Dropbox. Upload videos to YouTube or Vimeo and send us the link.

We accept body of works made with any kind of medium (text, photography, video, illustrations, animations, musics, etc). Edit tight. Make your work easy for us to see in an online gallery (you can make it private if you want). Include a short explanatory text.

For any other information, you can reach us at: postinternetera@gmail.com
